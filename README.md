# Mimita Remember 🛍️

Mimita Remember is an e-commerce website created for a family business to showcase and sell traditional food and handmade products.

## Features
- Product catalog
- Online presence for a small business
- Simple and user-friendly design
- Focus on customer experience

## Technologies
- HTML
- CSS
- Basic web development

## Project Purpose
This project was developed to help a family business expand its digital presence and reach more customers online.

## Impact
- Improved visibility of the business
- Created a digital platform for products
- Supported business growth through technology
